Terms & Conditions Step 3/4
Review & Agree
Please read and accept before using ResQ.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using ResQ, you agree to be bound by these terms. ResQ is an AI-powered emergency safety application designed to help users in distress situations.

2. Emergency Services

ResQ is not a replacement for official emergency services (Police: 100, Ambulance: 108, Fire: 101). Always contact official services first in life-threatening situations.

3. AI Audio Detection & Privacy

ResQ's AI model processes audio entirely on-device. No raw audio is ever transmitted to our servers. Only alert metadata (GPS, timestamp, confidence score) is shared. Audio snippets are opt-in and AES-256 encrypted.

4. Data & Privacy

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your personal information. Location data is only shared with your designated emergency contacts during an SOS event.

5. SOS Alert Usage

False SOS alerts are strictly prohibited. Repeated false alarms may result in account suspension. ResQ may report persistent false alarms to authorities.

6. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for keeping credentials secure, maintaining up-to-date emergency contacts, and using the app responsibly.

7. Limitations of Liability

ResQ shall not be held liable for network outages, device failures, or GPS inaccuracies beyond our control.

8. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes. Significant changes will be communicated via email.
